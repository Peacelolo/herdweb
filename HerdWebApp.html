What Sets Us Apart

HerdTrace stands out in the livestock management industry because of our commitment to innovation and security. Unlike traditional methods of livestock tracking, our solution uses advanced technology, including GPS-enabled ear tags and a mobile app, to offer real-time monitoring of your herd. Our Core Features

Real-time Tracking: Keep track of your livestock anywhere, anytime, with real-time GPS updates. Offline Capability: Our app works offline, ensuring that you can track your herd even in areas with limited internet connectivity. Health Monitoring: Track the health and well-being of your livestock with in-depth reports and alerts. Smart Ear Tags for Easy Tracking

Our durable ear tags contain a unique identification number linked to our database. The tag is securely attached to the animal’s ear, ensuring quick identification and monitoring.

Health Monitoring with Microchips

The embedded microchip continuously tracks vital health data, including temperature and movement. This helps detect early signs of illness and improves livestock management.
