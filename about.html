About HerdTrace

HerdTrace is committed to revolutionizing the way farmers manage their livestock. With cutting-edge technology, we offer solutions that ensure the safety and well-being of your herd, making livestock management more efficient and secure.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative and reliable livestock tracking solutions that empower farmers to protect their animals and improve their productivity. We aim to make livestock management seamless, easy, and secure for farmers across the globe.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology enhances the way farmers manage livestock, reducing theft, improving health management, and promoting sustainability in farming practices.
